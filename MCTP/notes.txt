To compile use:
g++ for cpp and gcc for c files.
```c
clang -Xpreprocessor -fopenmp test.c -I/usr/local/opt/libomp/include -L/usr/local/opt/libomp/lib -lomp -o test.x
```
No usar -fopenmp
```c
clang pi_v1.c -I/usr/local/opt/libomp/include -L/usr/local/opt/libomp/lib -lomp -o pi_v1.x
```
Simple compilacion:
```c
clang pi_v1.c -I/usr/local/opt/libomp/include -o pi_v1.x
```
Ejecutar Python:(Activar env)
mpirun --oversubscribe -np 4 python3 pi_mpi.py

Compilar MPI:
mpicc -fopenmp pi_mpi.c -o pi_mpi.x  # o sin -fopenmp
run:
export OMP_NUM_THREADS=2 
mpirun -np 2 pi_mpi.x
mpirun --oversubscribe -np 4 pi_mpi.x
